ABOUT US

We are EZY Solar Ltd – a forward thinking team, committed to bringing you green, reliable energy at an affordable price.

OUR STORY

We are EZY Solar Ltd, a newly established family business based in Accra, Ghana. We are passionate about green, clean energy and are eager to see Ghana utilise the abundant renewable, natural resources at its disposal, to better serve Ghanaians and the environment. The use of solar energy would be a direct response to the growing need for alternative green energy supplies – a solution to the existing electricity supply problem in Ghana. We want to make solar energy readily available, and accessible to the average Ghanaian. We believe that one of the best ways to achieve this is by making solar energy systems affordable.
